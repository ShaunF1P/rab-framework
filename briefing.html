🏆 Victory Lap & Brag Receipt

A viral sharing engine that transforms assessment results into downloadable, social-media-ready infographics.

Select Wins → Generate Receipt → Download PNG → Share on Socials
💡 Why This Matters
Every shared brag receipt is a free advertisement. The equation format (Win + Win = Decision) is designed to be inherently shareable and encourage engagement.

🔗 GoHighLevel Lead Pipeline

Every email capture triggers a three-pronged data flow:

📧 Email Entered → Supabase DB → Cloud Run Relay → GHL Contact + Tags
  • Universal tag: leap-score
  • Decision-specific tags: leap-vacation, leap-house, leap-marriage, etc.
  • Enables segmented nurture campaigns and partner data licensing
